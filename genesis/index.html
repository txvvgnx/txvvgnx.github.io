What is a VPN?

A VPN, or Virtual Private Network is a secure and encrypted tunnel that connects your device to the internet. VPNs help protect your data from prying eyes, hackers and even your ISP. They're also great tools for unblocking censored websites, so that you can enjoy content from anyhwere in the world.
